How to register
1

Visit Play Football

Head to the Play Football website — Australia's official football registration platform used by Football Victoria.

2

Create or log in to your account

Create a free Play Football account, or log in if you already have one from a previous season.

3

Find Creswick Soccer Club

Search for "Creswick Soccer Club" and select your team — Seniors, Juniors or MiniRoos.

4

Complete your registration

Fill in your details, pay the registration fee and you're done. We'll be in touch with next steps.
